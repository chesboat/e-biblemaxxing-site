How it works

01 — LOCK

Your apps get a doorpost

Choose the apps that pull at you. Open one, and a short passage of Scripture stands at the door — powered by Apple's Screen Time, entirely on your device.

02 — READ

Read, then enter

One small passage — short enough to truly sit with. Tap "I've read it," and you're in. Your apps stay open for a window you choose.

03 — BECOME

Every unlock adds up

A daily portion paces you through all 66 books — 73 with the Catholic canon — with streaks, milestones, and a finish date with your name on it.

The app

Built different

  • The whole Bible, on your unlocks. Reading plans from Genesis-to-Revelation to chronological to the Great Story — plus Gospels-first on-ramps and focus plans.
  • Catholic canon included. Douay-Rheims with all 73 books, alongside KJV and World English Bible. All offline.
  • A daily portion that bends, never breaks. Miss a day and tomorrow asks a little more — capped, always. No guilt mechanics. Ever.
  • Lock intensity you choose. Gentle, Committed (first unlock waits for your reading), or Strict (apps stay locked until today's portion is done).
  • Milestones worth keeping. Finish books, testaments, the whole Bible — and earn a certificate worth framing.
  • Radically private. No account. No server. No analytics. Your reading stays between you and God.
